EVENT SUMMARY

What is the current state of 3D printing?What are the latest industrial printers and what are the materials we can print with today?This interactive seminar aims to educate the industrial design world on the current state of 3D printing and how it can be applied to industries today.
什么是3D打印？最先进的3D打印机能生产什么？我们的设计私房课通过互动的交流，与大家分享3D打印最新的科技进展，并展示工业设计中的创意应用，希望能够激发设计师无尽的创造力。
